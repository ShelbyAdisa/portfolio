About Rihanna

Robyn Rihanna Fenty also known as Rihanna, was born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados.
Rihanna is a globally renowned singer, songwriter, actress, and entrepreneur.
She gained her first international recognition in 2005 with her debut album Music of the Sun, and her hit single "Pon de Replay."
Over the years, she has established herself as one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
